about us

Faracare is a healthcare provider that offers a wide range of medical services to its patients. They are dedicated to providing high-quality care and ensuring that their patients receive the best possible treatment.Their services include primary care, specialist consultations, diagnostic testing, and a range of medical procedures. Our special services

Faracare offers telemedicine services for virtual consultations and a dedicated team for managing chronic diseases, as well as a range of preventive health screenings for early health issue identification. With a patient-centered approach and the use of latest medical technologies, Faracare strives to provide high-quality care for its patients.

Sick Care Services

Faracare provides comprehensive care for patients who are acutely ill or recovering from an injury or surgery. Their team of healthcare professionals work together to provide personalized care, monitor progress, and ensure a smooth and speedy recovery.

Geriatric Care

Faracare offers specialized care for the elderly, including support with daily activities, managing chronic conditions, and providing rehabilitation services.

Pediatric Developmental and Behavioral Assessment

Faracare offers developmental and behavioral assessments for children who may have developmental delays or behavioral concerns. This includes assessments for conditions such as autism spectrum disorder, attention deficit hyperactivity disorder, and learning disabilities.
